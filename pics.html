About Us

We are a creative events’ management company

A Passion for Excellence

Elizabeth R was incorporated in 2003, as a public relations and events management company. We acknowledge our role within this dynamic environment as being able to differentiate between the exceptional and an ordinary event.

We are committed to fulfilling our clients’ needs and expectations through unsurpassed services and creative thinking. Our  beliefs have always been honesty, credibility, and integrity and trust.

We strive to always deliver this to our clients, sponsors, and attendees of every event.
